This is a website, and that's a picture of me! Hello world! I made this website in 2018 to document my summer and the things I did that year, but I was originally assigned this as a project for school

Why Little Fictions?

I'm sure you're wondering why I picked such an unrelated name for my website, I decided on "Little Fictions" for a multitude of reasons, some more complex than others:

  • I had decided to start building my website and soon found that I needed a name for it. As I'm not exactly the best with names, I fired up Spotify and started listening to "Firebrand and Angel" by Elbow; thinking that it was such a good song, I decided to listen to the album, "Little Fictions". The title song played and I was transported to another world.I had nothing else to name my website, so I mostly used it as a placeholder at first, being so intrigued by the song. While trying thinking of new names, I instead found a reason to keep it.

  • I felt that the name "Little Fictions" was sort of indicitive of my summer, being full of little fictions. In a more literal sense, the project that I chose for myself was to write a collection of short stories, which are in essence, little fictions.
