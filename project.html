My Summer Project

June 26th-September 5th

Original Plans

When first tasked with picking a summer project for my Software Engineering class, I thought that studying aerodynamics and aerodynamics would be interesting. I soon after created a requirements document for it and decided that I would write a research paper that would be followed by a virtual representation of a wind tunnel on Scratch. Soon after starting my research however, I found that I wasn't as passionate about the topic as I'd hoped.

New Requirements

After pondering on what to do for some time, I decided that I should do something that I'm passionate about. I remember thinking how I wanted to write more during the school year, so that's exactly what I made my project about.

With a brand new topic meant a new set of requirements needed to be written. Although I wanted to tap into my creative writing side, I also wanted to do something more productive at the same time, so I chose to incorperate that into my project. The requirements I wrote had two major parts: to write 4 or more short stories accompanied by a draft of my college essay.
